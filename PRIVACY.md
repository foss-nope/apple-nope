# Privacy Policy

Nope respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

Nope uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information. This helps improve the app by understanding which features are used and how the app performs.

Firebase Analytics may collect information such as:

- App interactions
- Device type and operating system
- General usage statistics

This information is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

## Crash Reporting

Nope uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information when the app crashes. This helps identify bugs and improve app stability.

Crash reports may include information such as:

- Device model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Stack traces at the time of the crash

This information does not personally identify you.

## Clipboard

When you copy a reason in the app, it is placed on your device’s clipboard using standard iOS functionality. Nope does not store or transmit clipboard contents.
